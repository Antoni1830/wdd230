🗺️Venezuela

Venezuela, officially the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela, is a sovereign country located in the northern part of South America and the Caribbean, made up of a continental area and a large number of islands and islets in the Caribbean Sea, whose capital and largest urban agglomeration is the city of Caracas.

🌽CACHAPA

Cachapa is a typical corn-based dish from Venezuela. Due to migratory exchanges with Venezuela, it has also spread to the Canary Islands. It is cooked in Budare.
